Recipe Card






INGREDIENTS
  • 475ml - Flour
  • 400ml - Sugar
  • 2kg - Butter
  • 5 eggs
  • 5ml - nutmeg
  • 5ml - cinamon
  • 5ml - cloves
  • 225ml - applesauce
  • 300g - mixed fruit
  • 225g - chopped pecans
  • 225g - chopped black walnuts
  • 225g - raisins
  • 125g - chopped dates






Note:

You can change the additional ingredients or its your choice to not add or add them!

METHOD

  1. Preheat the oven to 325 degrees.

  2. Mix flour, sugar, butter and eggs in the bowl.

  3. Add nutmeg, cinamon , cloves , applesauce , mixed fruit, chopped pecans , chopped black walnuts , raisins and chopped dates in the mixture. Make sure to add them one by one not putting all together.

  4. Pour the batter/mixture into 3 9 - inch pan that are greased and floured.

  5. Bake at 325 degrees for 35mins or until done.

  6. Cool on racks and enjoy it!!
